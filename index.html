Basic Electronics Explained

Welcome to a simple guide to basic electronics! Here, we'll cover some fundamental components and concepts.

Resistors

What they do:

Resistors limit the flow of electrical current in a circuit. They're measured in Ohms (Î©).

Formula: $V = IR$ (Ohm's Law, where V is voltage, I is current, and R is resistance)

Capacitors

What they do:

Capacitors store electrical energy in an electric field and release it when needed. They're measured in Farads (F).

They are used in filtering and timing circuits.

LEDs (Light Emitting Diodes)

What they do:

LEDs emit light when an electrical current passes through them. They are diodes, allowing current to flow in one direction only.

It is important to use a resistor in series with an LED to prevent it from burning out.
